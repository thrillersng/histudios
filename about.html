About Us

Hi-Impact Studios is a subsidiary of Solution Media and Infotech, situated on the outskirts of Lagos, Nigeria. It is the umbrella body of Hi-Imapct TV and Hi-Impact Radio with an essence of education and entertainment from which the pay-off line “securing the future” was coined.

Hi-Impact TV is the first full HD station in Nigeria, transmitting to over 79 countries in the world, quality entertainment programmes with an array of exceptional international titles, captivating telenovelas, indigenously produced contents that celebrate the African culture of dance, drama and music from different African ethnic groups, and a few others from other non-African climes.

Our fully equipped television and radio stations have the finest set designs and world class production facilities for recordings and transmission. Hi-Impact Studios has the best technical quality for the production of both local and international content.
